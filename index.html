Social Security is user-entered and simplified; it does not estimate real SSA benefits. Foreign tax credit and treaty treatment are simplified. Tax-payment 401k pulls are simplified to avoid recursive tax calculations and can slightly understate year-of-dry-up tax in edge cases. Educational only.
